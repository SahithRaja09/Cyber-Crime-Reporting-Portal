About

The aim of the Online Cyber Crime Reporting System is to develop a web-based platform that enables people to report crimes online efficiently. This system facilitates the sharing of crime scene details to ensure prompt action by the police. Additionally, it provides information on missing persons, most wanted criminals, and security tips to enhance public awareness.
